My Favorite Restaurants in Saipan

I lived in Saipan for most of my life and to live is to eat and where are we eating? Well let me introduce to you to some of favorite restaurants. Whether your craving for some island classics, perhaps something ready to go, or maybe you're in the mood for some Italian-Inspired cuisine—Saipan has got it all!

Top 3 Places to Dine

  1. Himawari
  2. Shirley's Coffee Shop
  3. The Angry Penne

Himawari | Garapan, Saipan

Himawari is one of my go to places when it comes to food. They provide baked goods, ready to eat bento, and there is even a hotel! (and also a convenience store) If you're in a rush Himawari is a great place to grab some sushi, drinks, and maybe a donut while you're at it. The only thing that you should be wary off is getting there at a reasonable time so that you have a better selection of bento's and watch out for the parking because it is busy!

Must Try:

  • Seared Salmon Ikura Bowl
  • Blueberry Cream Bread
  • Tempura Zaru Udon
